# Sprint Brief: S84-T1 -- App Information Content Rights

## Context

App Store Connect requires Content Rights Information in App Information before review. Fintheon uses or displays third-party-adjacent market, news, chart, AI, and integration content, so this track must inventory the content rights position before an Admin fills the field.

## Linear Organization

- Issue naming: `S84-T1: App Information content rights`
- Linear issue: `SOL-237`
- Parent: `SOL-233`
- Brief path: `@sprint-md/S84-T1-app-information-content-rights.md`
- Project: `Beta -- Mobile PWA`
- Cycle: `8 - Beta Closed`
- Owner: TP/Admin
- Execution path: App Store Connect

## Scope

- [ ] Inventory content surfaced in the app: market data, news/RiskFlow items, charts, AI output, uploaded user content, and connected third-party services.
- [ ] Decide the truthful App Store Connect Content Rights answer.
- [ ] Enter Content Rights Information in App Information.
- [ ] Record the final field value and any rights assumptions in the Linear issue.

## Acceptance

- [ ] Content Rights Information is complete in App Store Connect.
- [ ] The answer does not claim Fintheon owns or has rights to third-party content unless that is documented.
- [ ] If an unresolved rights dependency exists, review submission remains blocked and the dependency is named.
