Privacy Policy

Effective June 7, 2026. Last updated May 29, 2026. This policy applies to the Fintheon Early Research Preview, the July 1, 2026 beta release, and related websites, applications, services, and support channels operated by Priced In Research.

1. Who We Are

Fintheon is an integrated trading research environment developed by Priced In Research. The service helps users organize market data, research notes, agent-generated analysis, alerts, watchlists, and related workflow context. Fintheon is research and decision-support software; it is not a broker-dealer, exchange, investment adviser, or trade execution venue.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including contact details, account credentials or authentication identifiers, waitlist submissions, support messages, settings, prompts, uploaded files or screenshots, research notes, watchlists, desk plans, alerts, agent approvals, and other content you choose to enter into Fintheon.

We also collect operational information needed to run the service, including device and browser metadata, IP address, approximate location derived from network data, app version, diagnostic logs, crash reports, usage events, local storage state, and security signals. If you connect third-party services, trading tools, market data accounts, calendars, communication tools, or AI providers, we may process the information needed to operate those integrations.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to provide and secure Fintheon, authenticate users, deliver research and alerting features, personalize workspaces, process support requests, improve reliability, detect abuse, debug incidents, maintain audit trails, and prepare aggregate product analytics. We may use market context, prompts, and workflow content to generate AI-assisted analysis for the requesting user or workspace.

4. AI, Market Data, And Research Content

Fintheon may send prompts, uploaded content, research context, agent memory, and relevant market information to configured AI, cloud, infrastructure, analytics, and market-data providers so the requested feature can work. AI output can be incomplete, delayed, or wrong. Users remain responsible for reviewing all research and trading decisions.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell personal information. We share information with service providers that help us host, secure, monitor, support, and operate Fintheon; with integrations you choose to connect; with professional advisers when needed; and when required by law, security, compliance, or protection of rights. Workspace content may be visible to authorized members of the same workspace according to the roles and permissions configured for that workspace.

6. Cookies And Local Storage

Fintheon uses cookies, local storage, and similar technologies for authentication, preferences, app state, security, diagnostics, and product functionality. Blocking these technologies may prevent parts of the service from working.

7. Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service, support beta operations, preserve audit and security records, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve reliability. We may retain de-identified or aggregate information that no longer identifies a user.

8. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information, including authentication, access controls, monitoring, and encrypted transport where appropriate. No system is perfectly secure, and users should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive information.

9. Your Choices And Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, export, or restriction of personal information by contacting us. Some requests may be limited by security, legal, audit, or operational requirements. If you are in a jurisdiction with additional privacy rights, we will respond according to applicable law.

10. Children

Fintheon is intended for adults and is not directed to children. Do not use Fintheon if you are under 18 or otherwise unable to enter into these terms under applicable law.

11. Changes

We may update this policy as Fintheon moves from Early Research Preview to beta and later releases. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

12. Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to contact@pricedinresearch.io.
