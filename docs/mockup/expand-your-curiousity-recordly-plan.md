# Recordly Shooting Plan — Fintheon "Expand Your Curiousity"

Satirical product mockup for the new Fintheon situational-awareness and research surfaces.

**Working title:** `Expand Your Curiousity`  
**Spelling:** keep `curiousity` as supplied for the satirical title-card unless changed later.  
**Target cut:** 60-75s primary, with a 30s cutdown possible.  
**Format:** record 16:9 desktop first. We can crop later for vertical/social if needed.  
**Tone:** same pain-point lane as the prior Receipts mockups: funny because the trader's current research stack is obviously deranged, but the product answer is serious.

## Core joke

Retail research now means: 43 tabs, three Discord claims, a tweet screenshot, stale PDFs, a half-read Substack, and one AI answer with no receipts.

Fintheon turns that chaos into:

1. situational awareness,
2. evidence-backed narrative research,
3. visible agent deliberation,
4. source/contradiction tracking,
5. a routing gate for what deserves human attention.

## Pain points to preserve from the Receipts lane

- "Receipts" are scattered screenshots, not a usable evidence trail.
- Everyone says "look into this"; nobody says what changed, why now, or what contradicts it.
- AI chat gives a confident paragraph, then disappears with the context.
- News feeds shout faster than a human can connect cause/effect.
- A trader has to pay the attention tax, terminal tax, tab tax, and memory tax.
- The actual question is not "what happened?" It is "what matters, what connects, and what should I do with it?"

### What it should communicate

- Market open is noisy and social.
- Everyone is reacting to the same tape/headlines from different seats.
- Fintheon does not pretend the zoo disappears; it gives the zoo structure.
- NarrativeFlow, research rails, and Forum Chat together show the future loop: community chatter becomes context, context becomes evidence, evidence becomes monitored narrative.
